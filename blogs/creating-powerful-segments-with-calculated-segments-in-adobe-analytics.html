Creating Powerful Segments with Calculated Segments in Adobe Analytics

Adobe Analytics offers Calculated Segments, a powerful tool for creating user groups based on specific criteria. This allows you to analyze website behavior for targeted audiences.

**Benefits of Calculated Segments:**

  • Target specific user groups for in-depth analysis.
  • Combine data points from Props, Evars, and other metrics for precise segmentation.
  • Create dynamic segments that update automatically based on user behavior.

**Creating Calculated Segments:**

  1. Go to the Segmentation workspace in Adobe Analytics.
  2. Click 'New Segment' and choose 'Calculated Segment'.
  3. Define your segment criteria using conditions and operators on various data points.
  4. Save your segment for future use.

Calculated Segments empower you to gain deeper insights into specific user behavior, leading to better website optimization and marketing strategies.
